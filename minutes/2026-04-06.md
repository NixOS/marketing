            the quick recap is that we can't cname on the domain apex (nixos.org), but we could on www.nixos.org

            that broke at least once and I lost trust that the DNS provider can reliable provide that functionality

            most of all I want your input on whether switching the canonical name from nixos.org to www.nixos.org will meaningfully hurt SEO
            if that is even relevant
            I would feel much more comfortable if we did that because it relaxes the constraints we have on DNS hosting.
