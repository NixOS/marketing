# Swag

Logos are available at https://github.com/NixOS/nixos-artwork/

A standard recommended "swag pack" to have a Nix booth at a conference:
- 200 stickers
- large banner or sign
- flyers/brochures/handouts
- items to sell: (potentially)
    - t-shirts
    - mugs
